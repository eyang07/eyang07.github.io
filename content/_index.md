I am a junior studying Computer Engineering and Pure Mathematics at Northwestern University. My research interests are primarily in formal verifications, particularly in automated theorem proving, type theory, and the Curry-Howard correspondence. 

Currently, I am a research assistant at the IDEAS Lab, where I research LLM-assisted theorem proving in Interactive Theorem Provers (ITPs). I also contribute to DeepMind's Formal Conjectures and Mathlib through the Northwestern Lean Lab.

Previously, I interned at Deepthink.ai. I finished my A levels in Further Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry from Brighton College.
